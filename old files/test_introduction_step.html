Experiment Introduction

  1. In the next step, you are asked to play a very small game.
    • You need to click each ball until all of them disappear.
    • The total time should be more than 30 seconds.
  2. After the small game, you are asked to watch a short lecture video.
    • During watching the video, you need to keep you position stable.
    • Most operations are allowed, except for speeding up and skipping the video playing.
